About Justin Bieber

Today, Justin Bieber is an influential musician in modern day pop music. He began his career after being discovered by marketing executive Scooter Braun after accidentally clicking on one of Justin Bieber's ealry YouTube videos when looking for another artist. He released his first single, "One Time" in 2008 and the rest is history. Justin Bieber has cited his musical influences to be Michael Jackson, Mariah Carey, Usher, Justin Timberlake, Stevie Wonder, among others.

Discography

2010-2012: My World 2.0 and Believe

Justin Bieber's debut album My World 2.0 was released in 2010 and features international hits like "Baby" and "Somebody to Love". This album earned a Grammy nomination for Best Pop Vocal ALbum. Justin's second studio album was a Christmas album that paved the way for his hit third album, Believe debuting in 2012. This album includes hit singles like Boyfriend, Beuaty and a Beat, and As Long As You Love Me.

Purpose

Purpose debuted in 2015. This fourth studio album featured four promotional singles: "What Do You Mean", "Sorry", "Love Yourself", and "Company". The album was nominated for Best Pop Vocal ALbum and Album of the Year at the 2017 Grammy Awards. The album's song Love Yourself received two nominations as well, Song of the Year and Best Pop Solo Performance.

Justice

Justin Bieber's most recent album, Justice, was released in late 2021 with six supporting singles, including hits like "Anyone" and "Hold On". This album received eight nominations at the 2022 Grammy Awards, including Album, Song, and Record of the Year. While working on this album Justin Bieber recorded a YouTube documentary called Justin Bieber: Next Chapter that offers an initimate look at the recording of the album during quarantine amidst the global pandemic.
